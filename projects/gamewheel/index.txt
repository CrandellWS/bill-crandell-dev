e:T20d5,<h1>GameWheel - Advanced Random Selection Spinner</h1>
<p>GameWheel is a powerful, open-source random selection spinner with multiple game modes, chat platform integration, and extensive customization options. Perfect for streamers, educators, event organizers, and anyone who needs fair random selection.</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<h3>üéÆ Multiple Game Modes</h3>
<p><strong>First Win Mode</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Traditional wheel behavior - spin and select a winner</li>
<li>Optional winner removal</li>
<li>Perfect for giveaways and simple selections</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Last Remaining Mode</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Competitive elimination format</li>
<li>Each spin eliminates the selected entry</li>
<li>The final entry is the ultimate winner</li>
<li>Best for tournaments and battle royale style events</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Full Tilt Mode</strong> (NEW in v2.2.0)</p>
<ul>
<li>Dramatic ladder-climbing competition</li>
<li>Players race to reach the top</li>
<li>Configurable heights: 3, 5, 7, or 10 rungs</li>
<li>Visual ladder display with real-time animated progress</li>
<li>Champion celebration with confetti and trophy overlay</li>
<li>All-or-nothing suspense and progressive competition</li>
</ul>
